Amazon's Influence and Global Presence

Amazon is a multinational technology company based in Seattle, Washington. It started as an online marketplace for books and has expanded to include a wide collection of products and services. Through its official website, Amazon provides an extensive range of goods, including electronics, apparel, groceries, and digital content, as well as cloud computing services like Amazon Web Services (AWS).

Amazon's impact on global retail and technological innovation has been immense. Its customer-centric approach into various industries have propelled its growth and made it one of the most succesful companies in the world.
